What to Do in an Earthquake

Step 1: Drop, Cover, Hold On

Get down, take cover under sturdy furniture, and hold on.

Step 2: Stay Indoors

Remain inside until shaking stops. Move away from windows.

Step 3: After Shaking

Carefully exit the building, watching for hazards.

Step 4: Listen for Updates

Use a battery-powered radio for emergency information.

Step 5: Help Others

Check for injuries and provide first aid if needed.
