What to Do in a Road Accident

Step 1: Stop

Safely stop your vehicle and turn on hazard lights.

Step 2: Call for Help

Contact emergency services if there are injuries.

Step 3: Warn Others

Use warning triangles or flares if available.

Step 4: Provide First Aid

Assist injured persons if you can do so safely.

Step 5: Document

Exchange information and document the scene if safe.
