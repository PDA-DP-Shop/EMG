What to Do in a Gas Leak

Step 1: Smell Gas?

Do not use electrical switches or flames.

Step 2: Ventilate

Open windows and doors to let gas escape.

Step 3: Evacuate

Leave the area immediately.

Step 4: Call Emergency

Contact gas company or emergency services from a safe location.

Step 5: Do Not Re-enter

Wait for professionals to declare the area safe.

Emergency Numbers

  • Gas Emergency: 1906
  • Fire Department: 101
  • Ambulance: 102
