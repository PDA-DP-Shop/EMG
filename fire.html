Fire Emergency

Step 1: Stay Calm

Do not panic. Assess the situation quickly.

Step 2: Evacuate

Leave the building immediately using the nearest safe exit.

Step 3: Use Extinguisher

If safe, use a fire extinguisher to put out small fires.

Step 4: Call Emergency

Dial emergency services once you are safe.

Step 5: Do Not Re-enter

Never go back inside a burning building.

Emergency Numbers

  • Fire Department: 101
  • Ambulance: 102
  • Police: 100
  • General Emergency: 112
