First Aid Steps

Step 1: Assess the Situation

Ensure the area is safe before helping.

Step 2: Call for Help

Contact emergency services if needed.

Step 3: Stop Bleeding

Apply pressure to wounds with a clean cloth.

Step 4: Clean Wounds

Rinse with clean water if available.

Step 5: Monitor

Keep the person comfortable and monitor their condition.
